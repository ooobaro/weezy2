ABOUT US

OVER

YEARS

OF PROFESSIONAL CIVIL
ENGINEERING

Jefcon & Associates Ltd is a Civil Engineering Consulting Firm with high precision GNSS
data acquisition solutions with capacities for delivering various Civil Engineering Design services,
including Flood Vulnerability Studies, Modeling and Design of flood control infrastructures, Building
and Civil/Structural engineering, high precision Surveying and mapping. With the experiences of
highly trained Professional Engineers and Consultants pulled together, Jefcon provides high
precision Mechanical, Electrical, Water Resources and Environmental Engineering services to Public,
Private, Local and International economies. The company also provides Industrial Support Services
to Oilfield production and facilities as well as Consultancy services in Project & Facilities
Management.

OUR SERVICES

Engineering Design and Construction

We find creative ways to solve real-world problems and
develop the physical infrastructure for society to exist -
from bridges to houses, and from roads to hospitals and
train stations.
We apply high-level civil engineering principles alongside
analytical, practical and engineering skills and techniques.

Environmental Impact Studies and Management

We are experts in environmental impact studies and management,
carried out in two major folds:
We perform Environmental impact assessment (EIA) as a statutory
procedure to define the environmental impacts of a project, as well
as to promote communication to and involvement of citizens, even
in the early stages of a project.

Engineering Surveys

We carry out those activities that are involved in the planning and
execution of surveys for the development, design, construction,
operation and maintenance of civil and other engineered projects.
Our Engineering survey is a specialty job within the broader
professional practice of engineering and includes all surveying
activities required to support the conception, planning, design,
construction, maintenance, and operation of engineered projects.

Advisory Services in Engineering

We ultilise our reservoire of experience for consulting services
from which we develop findings, conclusions and
recommendations that are presented to the client for consideration
and decision making. Expert advice on the design, planning and
management of civil construction projects, covering all types of
residential and commercial construction, particularly in the area of
larger public infrastructure projects.

Project Supervision and Management

Our function in your project management is to ensure that the project
objectives are achieved within the planned budget and schedule. Our
methods ensure, among other things, the smooth continuation of the
project while the project manager is away or if the project manager is
replaced.

Project Costing, Valuation and Tender analysis

With a seasoned team of Quantity Surveyors who can
provide a full system of budget estimate and cost planning,
expenditure is controlled and clients receive better value for
money in both design & construction, and project costs are
kept within an agreed budget. We also assist the architect
with comparative costs for alternative systems of
construction or finishing, as well as advise on the total cost
implications of particular methods of construction.

HEALTHY AND SAFETY
ENVIRONMENT

The basic aim of HSE plans, programmes and objectives of Jefcon & Associates Ltd is to create and maintain a high standard of safety awareness among employees and visitors to the work site and to protect the present environment.
In implementing the HSE policy, members of staff are encouraged to pay appropriate
regards to the environment and its habitats.
The company shall adopt stronger measures to ensure that injuries to personnel and
property damage to plants and equipment are completely eliminated or reduced to a
minimum level.

POLICIES

Our Company is committed to rendering quality services to her clients’ satisfaction with a Total Quality Management (TMQ) approach that meets local and international standard in Building, Civil, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical, Information Technology, Water Resources, Environmental and Waste Management services/maintenance. We believe in the concept of total quality in ensuring clients’ satisfaction, her employees and the clients’ work together in pursuit of common objective.
JEFCON & ASSOCIATES LTD believes that the engine of Business is Customer Driven, thus our business transactions are based on the following:

  1. we believe that the Safety and Security of our Employees,Clients/Clients Representatives and Third Parties is as important as
       the business itself.

  2. We believe and apply the Total Quality Management in all facets of our operations.

  3. We develop and use qualified personnel, trained with necessary skills and experience to perform their duties.

  4. We constantly review our Environmental Management Plan to reflect the current demands of our operations.

  5. We open communication with clients and suppliers to ensure awareness and maintenance of project quality standards and
       objectives.

  6. We believe that, for every project, Waste Management is as important as the final output of the job.

  7. We perform in accordance with the documented Quality management System with the aim of error free performance.

  8. We encourage the participation of all stakeholders in our operations.

  9. We believe business cannot thrive while the resources upon which the business is based dies.

JEFCON & ASSOCIATES LTD is a benchmark company in engineering services, construction and safety within the engineering industries in Nigeria. We are committed to maintaining impeccable, professional and ethical standards in all our business dealings. We will always negotiate in good faith with the intent of fostering long term, mutually beneficial relations with our customers, employees and suppliers.

Community Relations

Over the years we have devised appropriate methods to enhance cordial relationship between the host community and the company. In furtherance of this objective we intend to carry out the following during the course of executing any project:

  1. To respect and abide by all native laws and custom.

  2. To raise community awareness and show high sense of commitment to the community where we are operating.

  3. To provide employment for host community at every location whenever possible.

  4. To improve on our effort on community assistance.

  5. To ensure no pollution in all areas of our operation.

  6. To ensure appropriate disposal of domestic and industrial waste generated.

  7. To ensure all operation are carried out in a safe manner and eliminate the possibility of causing injury to the community,             workers and environment.

  8. To ensure all negotiation and any other dealing with the host community are conduct with the knowledge and constant of the         company.

  9. To assist empowerment of people from the host community

  10. To source all materials that is available within the local community.
